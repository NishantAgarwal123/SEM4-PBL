TRIZ PRINCIPLES

Extraction:
By using solar panels we can extract the rays of the sun and utilize it in accordance with our requirement the energy absorbed is known as solar energy.

Universality:
The energy absorbed by the sun can be utilized in performing multiple functions which can definitely save depletion of energy resources.

Continuous useful action:
The sun is shining for the whole day so the solar panels can absorb the energy for the whole day with full capacity and utilize it accordingly.

Equipotentiality:
Once the solar panels are installed they are not required to be raised or lowered, they will perform their work in an efficient manner.

Feedback:
We have absorbed sun's rays through solar panels if certain tasks are performed by utilizing that energy so it can be said that the solar panels are working properly and are facilitating maximum efficiency.
